Common Questions

Can citizens still submit complaints the same way? +

Yes. The flow still supports complaint submission, AI analysis, PDF generation, BBMP email, map preview, and permissions.

Does the admin dashboard still manage alerts and status changes? +

Yes. Alerts, complaint status updates, resets, and account management remain preserved through the same backend permissions.

How does voice input work now? +

Voice complaints use recorded audio plus backend transcription, then place the processed transcript into the complaint summary field.

About

What Urban Pulse does and how the app is organised.

See the issue. Follow the response.

Scroll to expand

See the issue. Follow the response.

Urban Pulse is a complaint reporting and management app. Citizens can submit issues with text, voice, location, and images. Admins can review complaints, update status, manage alerts, and handle users.

Report issues

Submit complaints with location, image evidence, text, and voice input.

Use AI support

Generate image descriptions and issue summaries to speed up reporting.

Take action

Generate PDFs, send email, track alerts, and update complaint status.

Main sections

  • Home: overview of the product and main flows.
  • Report: upload evidence, fill the form, and submit a complaint.
  • Complaints: review submitted complaints and their updates.
  • Dashboard: admin metrics, complaint management, and actions.
  • Alerts: items that need attention.
  • Map: location preview and user management access.

Built for

  • Citizens who want a simple reporting flow.
  • Admins who need a single place to review and act.
  • Teams who want reporting, tracking, and escalation in one app.
